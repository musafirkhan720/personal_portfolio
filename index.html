About Me

I am Musafir Khan, currently pursuing a degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering at AWH Engineering College Calicut. Alongside my academic journey, I hold the esteemed position of Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) at the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) of AWH. I am also actively involved as a volunteer for the National Service Scheme (NSS). My educational background includes studying Electronics and Communication Engineering at KMCT Polytechnic College, where I completed my diploma in the field. Prior to that, I completed my higher secondary education at N.G.O Quarters Govt. Higher Secondary School, specializing in Computer Science. My educational journey began at JDT Islam High School, where I completed my high school education. Within my family, I am blessed with the love and support of my parents, as well as three sisters, all of whom are happily married and pursuing successful careers in their respective professions. Overall, I strive to maintain an elegant approach to my studies and professional endeavors, always seeking to expand my knowledge and contribute meaningfully to the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering.
